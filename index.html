Not Mastermind

Welcome to Not Mastermind.

The goal of this game is to guess the order of four random colors. A color may be used more than once. By the pigeonhole principle (sorry), not every color is used.

After each guess, 0 to 4 pips will appear to the left of your guess. An unfilled pip denotes a correct color in the wrong spot. A filled pip denotes a correct color in the correct spot. HOWEVER, the location of the pip does not correspond to a location of your guess.

Controls:

If a cell is selected (by clicking on an unfilled cell), clicking a color peg will fill that cell. Otherwise, the leftmost unfilled cell will be filled.

You can click on a filled cell to clear it.

You can type the character of the color peg instead of clicking a color peg, and you can type <enter> instead of clicking the submit button.

Refreshing the page will give you a new puzzle.
